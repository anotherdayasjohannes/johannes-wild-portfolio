## ðŸ’¡ Tips for Your Palette

For a dark theme portfolio, consider:

1. **Brand Colors**: 
   - Choose 1-2 vibrant colors for CTAs and highlights
   - Should pop against dark backgrounds
   - Good contrast for accessibility

2. **Backgrounds**: 
   - Very dark (but not pure black #000000)
   - Slight variations for depth
   - Consider: `#0A0A0A`, `#121212`, `#1A1A1A`

3. **Text Colors**:
   - Almost white for primary text (not pure white)
   - Gray scales for hierarchy
   - Consider: `#F5F5F5`, `#D1D1D1`, `#A1A1A1`

4. **Accents**:
   - Blue: Professional, tech
   - Purple: Creative, modern
   - Green: Growth, success
   - Orange: Energy, action
